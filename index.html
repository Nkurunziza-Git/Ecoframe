EcoFlame Energy Biogas Plant

Turning Waste into Wealth

Empowering Communities, Protecting the Environment

1. Challenges in Nyagatare District

  • Overwhelming organic waste from livestock and farms.
  • Dependency on unsustainable energy sources like firewood and charcoal.
  • Limited access to affordable fertilizers for farmers.
  • Environmental degradation and methane emissions.

2. Our Solution

The EcoFlame Biogas Plant:

  • Converts waste into renewable biogas for cooking, heating, and industrial use.
  • Produces high-quality organic fertilizer to improve agricultural productivity.
  • Addresses waste management challenges sustainably.

3. How It Works

  1. Waste Collection: Organic waste is gathered from farms and communities.
  2. Digestion: Microorganisms break down waste in a digester, producing biogas and slurry.
  3. Energy Output: Biogas is purified and stored for use.
  4. Fertilizer Production: Residual organic matter is converted into nutrient-rich fertilizer.
